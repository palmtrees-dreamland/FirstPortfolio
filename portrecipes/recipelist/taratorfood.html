Tarator

Description

Balkan food has some tasty dishes. This is a cold soup, or Tarator. Consumed mostly during summer, it is actually one of my fave dishes and I enjoy it all year round as a drinkable appetizer to accompany mains during lunch time.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • 1 plain yoghurt
  • Fresh dill
  • A bit of chopped garlic
  • A couple of walnuts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Steps

  1. Diced desire amount of cucumber, the smaller the better so you can chew on those little bits.

  2. Pour the yoghurt in a jug and whisk so it gets a bit liquid.

  3. Add the diced cucumber, a bit of garlic and walnuts.

  4. Stir and add some olive oil and dill.

  5. Finally, it is time to add some water. Now, it depends on you how thick you want it to be
